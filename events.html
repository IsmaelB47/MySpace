Music Night Extravaganza

🎶 Music Night Extravaganza Rocks Fort Wayne! 🎶

Fort Wayne, IN — Saturday, July 20th, 2025 | 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM
Get ready, Fort Wayne! This summer, the city will come alive with the vibrant sound of music,
dazzling lights, and an electrifying atmosphere as the Music Night Extravaganza takes over downtown.
Whether you're a fan of rock, jazz, R&B, or pop, this event promises something for everyone. Held at Freimann Square, the heart of the city will transform
into a festival of live performances from local bands and guest artists.
The night will feature food trucks, interactive art,
and a glowing crowd of music lovers coming together under the stars.

What to Expect:
🎸 Live Performances by Fort Wayne’s hottest bands
🎤 Open mic hour for aspiring local talent
🍔 Food & Drinks from the best local vendors
🕺 Dance zone, light shows & giveaways
Event Details:
📍 Location: Freimann Square, Fort Wayne, IN
🕖 Time: 7:00 PM – 11:00 PM
💵 Admission: Free & open to the public
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 All ages welcome!
So grab your friends, bring a blanket or a lawn chair, and join us for a night to remember.
The Music Night Extravaganza is more than just a concert — it’s
Fort Wayne’s summer celebration of sound, culture, and community.

Culture Festival

🎉 City of Fort Wayne Cultural Arts
CULTURE FEST 2025
📅 Date: May 17, 2025
🕕 Time: 6:00 PM
📍 Location: Promenade Park Amphitheater
📫 Address: 202 W Superior St., Fort Wayne, IN
🎟️ Admission: FREE
Celebrate Fort Wayne’s rich cultural diversity at Culture Fest 2025,
an evening of music, dance, food, and community! Hosted by the City of Fort Wayne Cultural Arts,
this vibrant event will highlight the unique traditions that make our city so special. Enjoy a wide array of entertainment and experiences including:
🎶 Live Music from Local & Regional Artists
🛍️ Vendors Showcasing Cultural Crafts
🍲 Authentic Global Cuisine
💃 Traditional Dances from Around the World
🥁 Interactive Drum Circles & Performances
Bring your friends, family, and neighbors
to enjoy this FREE outdoor celebration in the heart of downtown Fort Wayne! 🌐 For more information, visit fortwayneparks.org
Accessibility Information:
The City of Fort Wayne is committed to making events inclusive and accessible to all residents.
If you have questions about access or would like to request accommodations,
please contact the City Clerk’s Office at (260) 427-1201 at least 14 days prior to the event.
